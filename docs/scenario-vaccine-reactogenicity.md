# Vaccine Reactogenicity Scenario

## Intended Audience

- Safety reviewer
- Medical monitor
- Clinical scientist
- Clinical operations

## What This Scenario Demonstrates

- Short-window AE review
- Reactogenicity-style symptom timing
- Visit-oriented review walkthroughs
- Synthetic safety reporting examples

## What This Scenario Does Not Demonstrate

- A real vaccine protocol
- Medical causality adjudication
- Production safety database operations
- Submission-ready SDTM or ADaM
