# Safety Reviewer Quickstart

## Goal

Evaluate whether the sandbox supports safety review discussions with synthetic
data.

## Try This

1. Open the Subject Profile Reference App.
2. Review AE summary fields.
3. Identify serious, related, or severe AEs.
4. Compare lab abnormalities against AE timing.
5. Compare exposure changes and dose modifications against AE intervals.
6. Open the Safety Review report.

## Evaluate

- Are seriousness, relatedness, severity, and outcome visible?
- Can a reviewer see whether an AE is treatment-emergent?
- Can lab-linked AEs be discussed from the same screen?
- Does the exported report support a review meeting?
- Is the sign-off clearly a demo evidence marker, not a regulated electronic signature?
