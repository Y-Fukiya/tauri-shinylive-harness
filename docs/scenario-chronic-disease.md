# Chronic Disease Scenario

## Intended Audience

- Medical monitor
- Data manager
- Clinical scientist
- Statistician or programmer

## What This Scenario Demonstrates

- Longer-running visit patterns
- Vitals and laboratory trend review
- Concomitant medication context
- Data consistency review
- Synthetic subject-level reporting

## What This Scenario Does Not Demonstrate

- Real patient management
- Long-term clinical decision support
- Production clinical operations
- Submission-ready SDTM or ADaM
