# Clinical Review Exercises

Use these exercises with the bundled synthetic data only.

## Exercise 1: Lab Abnormality And AE Consistency

Open `SUBJ-001` and compare ALT/AST trends with AE timing.

Check:

- whether the AE start day is near the lab abnormality period
- whether the AE occurs during active exposure
- whether relatedness is easy to review
- whether the exported Safety Review report preserves the context

## Exercise 2: Exposure And AE Timeline

Pick a subject with a dose modification and compare exposure intervals with AE
intervals.

Check:

- whether treatment-emergent and post-treatment events are distinguishable
- whether dose modification timing is understandable
- whether the timeline supports review discussion

## Exercise 3: Data Quality Review

Open `reports/clinical-data-pack-validation.json`.

Check:

- required column coverage
- subject reference consistency
- visit reference consistency
- controlled terminology findings
- timeline consistency findings

## Exercise 4: Evidence Navigation

Open `reports/evidence-index.html`.

Check:

- whether required evidence is present
- whether hashes are visible
- whether review sign-off is clearly pending
- whether the clinical-use limitation is clear
