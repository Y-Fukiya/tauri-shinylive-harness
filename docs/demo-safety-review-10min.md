# 10-Minute Demo: Safety Review

## Audience

Safety reviewer, medical monitor, clinical scientist.

## Message

Synthetic safety scenarios let teams evaluate AE, lab, exposure, and report
workflows before using any real clinical data.

## Flow

1. Open the Subject Profile Reference App.
2. Review AE summary counts.
3. Filter mentally for serious, related, and severe events.
4. Review lab abnormalities near AE start days.
5. Compare exposure changes and dose modifications against AE intervals.
6. Open the Safety Review report.
7. Point to relatedness, seriousness, severity, and outcome fields.
8. Point to data pack hash and clinical-use limitation.
9. Open `reports/cdisc-bridge-preflight.json`.
10. Confirm the bridge is not submission-ready.

## Talk Track

The purpose is to evaluate safety review ergonomics and evidence generation
with synthetic data. The demo sign-off is not a regulated electronic signature.
