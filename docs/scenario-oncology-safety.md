# Oncology Safety Scenario

## Intended Audience

- Safety reviewer
- Medical monitor
- Clinical scientist
- Data manager

## What This Scenario Demonstrates

- Lab abnormality review
- Dose modification discussion
- Treatment-emergent AE review
- Concomitant medication review
- Subject-level timeline review

## What This Scenario Does Not Demonstrate

- A real oncology protocol
- RECIST or efficacy assessment
- Real-world clinical decision making
- Submission-ready SDTM or ADaM
- Production safety surveillance
